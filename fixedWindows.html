Fixed Windows

Add architectural interest with an arch or special shape window. These windows are available in many shapes and sizes with a variety of different grid patterns. Like picture windows, they are fixed and do not open, but are perfect for lighting stairwells & other dark areas of the home. While more work is required to construct the window wells, arch and circular windows can offer a visual impact to any room. Not only will they provide a stunning focal point, but they maximize the light and view. Arch and special shape windows can be combined with our other Premium Window products or can stand alone as an accent. Available for residential or commercial use and in bulk!
